Consulting

Executive advisory, leadership development, and applied organizational psychology.

Advisory for leaders navigating complex human systems.

I work with executives, boards, and senior teams on the questions that don’t have clean answers: how to lead through uncertainty, how to build cultures that hold up under pressure, and how to make decisions when evidence and values pull in different directions.

Where I help

  • Leadership & executive coaching grounded in organizational psychology
  • Culture diagnostics, team dynamics, and change management
  • Decision-making under uncertainty: structured frameworks for hard calls
  • People strategy, talent assessment, and selection science
  • Speaking, workshops, and facilitated leadership offsites
  • Responsible AI: bias and fairness audits, model evaluation, and governance for AI systems

How I work

Engagements typically begin with a short discovery conversation, followed by a tailored scope. I prefer small, high-trust engagements where I can do my best thinking alongside clients rather than hand off a deck.
