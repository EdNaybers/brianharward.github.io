Ethics

Applied ethics for technology, organizations, and public-interest decision-making.

Applied ethics for technology, organizations, and policy.

Ethics is not a brand exercise. Done well, it is a discipline for clearer thinking about consequences, obligations, and trade-offs. I help organizations engage seriously with the ethical dimensions of their work, from emerging technology to everyday workplace policy.

Engagements include

  • AI and emerging-technology ethics review
  • Organizational ethics audits and policy review
  • Frameworks for responsible decision-making
  • Education and facilitated discussion for leadership teams
  • Independent ethics advisory and board service

Stance

I take a practical, pluralistic view: useful ethics work draws on multiple traditions, is honest about uncertainty, and survives contact with operational reality.
